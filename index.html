Act_1

HOPS Contour - CNC Layout Creator



This script creates a Layout for CNC laser cutting based from a geometry and board size specified

Parameters:
  • Geometry - Specify the Object to be contoured (Closed Solid)(MM)
  • Material Thickness - Specify the thickness of the contour material(Float)(MM)
  • Bounding Curve - Specify the Boundary to lay the curves on (Closed Curve)(MM)
  • Spacing - Specify the Spacing between geometry for Laser cut (Float)(MM)

Three.Js Attribute

Final Project

SOLIHIYA



Solihiya is a parametric pavillion configurator based from the philippine sunburst weaving pattern of the same name. The aim of the configurator is to the give users feedback on implications of different parametric configurations to the pavilion like height, openings, and solar parameters. This also gives users insight on different shadow patterns and behaviours depending on vectors got from solar angles and times of day.
